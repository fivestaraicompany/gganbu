Features

Every barrier to conversation, removed

No account or central chat server. Core nearby chat is device to device; translation, purchases, and safety reports may use network or platform services.

📡

Direct Bluetooth connection

Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) discovers and connects nearby devices directly. Core chats do not pass through a central chat server or require a mobile data plan.

🌐

Optional translation

Connect an Ollama-compatible endpoint to translate messages into the recipient's language. The original and translated text are shown together; endpoint availability and privacy practices apply.

📱

iOS · Android compatible

iPhone and Android use the same nearby protocol. You choose whom to contact from visible nearby profiles—there is no random chat matching.

💞

Become GGanbu

Send an interest heart — when it's mutual, you become 'GGanbu' (best buddies). Get closer to people you click with.

🖼️

Photo sharing

Send photos as well as text, directly over Bluetooth. Share the moment right away.

🔒

Privacy and safety controls

Control when you are discoverable, delete local conversations, and block or report abusive users. GGanbu Chat is for adults aged 18 and older.

How it works

Three simple steps

Set up a profile, choose when to be visible, and stay in control of each connection.

Set up your profile

Create a profile, grant the Bluetooth or Nearby devices permissions needed for direct chat, and choose when to become discoverable.

Find people nearby

Discoverable adults in the same space — a café, library, or event — appear with an approximate proximity based on Bluetooth signal strength.

Choose and start chatting

Select a nearby profile to chat directly. If translation is configured and available, your message can also arrive in the recipient's language.

Supported languages

Different languages? No problem

When a compatible translation endpoint is configured, GGanbu Chat supports translated conversations between these languages.

🇰🇷한국어 🇺🇸English 🇯🇵日本語 🇨🇳简体中文 🇹🇼繁體中文 🇹🇭ไทย

Connect nearby, responsibly

GGanbu Chat is for adults 18+. Review the in-app community rules, share thoughtfully, and use block or report whenever something feels unsafe.
